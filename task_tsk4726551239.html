Task prompt — delivered verbatim to the model

Zinc Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd. is a company which is willing to perform a diversification. The Board of Directors (BOD) is clueless about best strategy to be adopted. They have hired two best external consultants to help them with this open request. Over a duration of 3 months, two consultants have already performed their own market assessments as well as investment-return analysis using industry sensing and manufacturing domain connects. The list of sub-steps considered across consultants include different combinations of Capex (Capital Expenditures), sales forecasting (using sales pipeline assessment) for product (0→1 stage), cash flows against the expected life of the manufacturing plant. Last week all of them submitted their recommendations to the board of directors. The BOD wants to know the best strategy amongst the two recommendations by external consultants with the intent to maximize the total probable net profits realized by the opted strategy. • Use Strategy A.pptx for detailed recommendations from McKinsey’s consultant. • Use Strategy B – Part 1.0.pptx & Strategy B – Part 2.0.pptx for detailed recommendation by Bain’s consultant. Strictly DO NOT browse the web for any information. Only rely on the internal files provided. Over the years, the base organizations of consultants have realized that the demand scenarios within the Zinc manufacturing industry strictly be categorized as either “High” or “Low”. There exist no other moderate or interim scenarios. There is a limitation to the benchmarked industry standard reports utilized for future analysis or projections. Hence, all consultants are confident of cash flow for next 7 years and the same timeline shall be strictly used for any decision analysis. All set-up, expansion related Capex, cash outflow are one time i.e., there is no staggered investment. Similarly, all the cash inflows are single shot at the year end, no staggering patterns observed. Do not hallucinate or use/assume datapoints by yourself. Strictly adhere to data points provided in this prompt. Create an output word document with name of the file as “Zinc Future Strategy - BOD” & include chosen strategy, rationale, realized net profits from each recommendation as well as explain the methodology & decision logics used to arrive at the final go-to strategy.
